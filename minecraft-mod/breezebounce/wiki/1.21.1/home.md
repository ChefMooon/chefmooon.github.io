The information on this wiki covers all Minecraft versions.

### Features

**Velcro Armour:** Provides the "Velcro" effect when wearing a full set. Allowing the player to stick to wool tagged blocks.

**Bounce Blocks:** Full Block, Stairs, Slabs, and Posts (16 colours)

**Inflation Machine:** A functional block that keeps a group of connected Bounce Blocks ‘Inflated’ while fueled.
