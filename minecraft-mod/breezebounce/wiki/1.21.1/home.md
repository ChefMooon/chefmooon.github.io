**Bounce Blocks:** Full Block, Stairs, Slabs, and Posts (16 colours)

Breeze Bounce supports [Fabric](https://fabricmc.net) and [NeoForge](https://neoforged.net) for Minecraft 1.21-1.21.1 and 1.21.2-1.21.3.
